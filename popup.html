Voice Tab Controller

Ready

Available Commands:

  • "Stop listening" - Stops voice control
  • "New tab" - Opens a new tab
  • "Close tab" - Closes current tab
  • "Next tab" - Switch to next tab
  • "Previous tab" - Switch to previous tab
  • "New window" - Opens new window
  • "Close window" - Closes current window
  • "Reload" or "Refresh" - Reloads current tab
  • "Duplicate tab" - Duplicates current tab
  • "Pin tab" - Pins/unpins current tab
  • "Mute tab" - Mutes/unmutes current tab
